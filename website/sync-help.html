🔄 How to Sync Your Data

Keep your watchlist and viewing history across all your devices — no account needed.

Saving Your Data

1 Click the 🔄 button

You'll find it in the top navigation bar on any page.

2 Click "Save My Stuff"

This saves your watchlist and recently watched videos to the cloud.

3 Write down your code

You'll get a 6-character code like K7X2M9. Write it down, take a screenshot, or save it somewhere safe. This is your key to get your data back.

Restoring Your Data

1 On your other device, click 🔄

Open the site on your new device and click the sync button.

2 Switch to the "Restore" tab

Click the Restore tab at the top of the popup.

3 Enter your code

Type in your 6-character code and click "Restore My Stuff". Your watchlist and history will appear instantly.

Good to Know

  • Codes expire after 30 days. Save a new code if you need to keep syncing.
  • No account needed. Just a code. That's it.
  • Your data is private. Only someone with your code can access it.
  • Works everywhere. Phone, tablet, laptop — any browser.
